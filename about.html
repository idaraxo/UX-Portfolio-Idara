About

My Story

My passion for design started back in 2014 as a design editor of my high school yearbook. After graduating, I continued to use the skills I learned to create posters and promotional materials for different activities I participated in throughout college. My interest in design was most prevalent to me when I began to use them to create visuals for the projects of my engineering courses. From here I did more research to learn more about the intersections between engineering, design, healthcare and technology.My interest further developed in 2020 as a student in CareerFoundry’s User Experience Design and User Interface course. I created a case study, the Sobered Health app, a resource for users recovering from addiction. My previous work has led me to develop a passion to create designs that make healthcare resources more accessible. When I am not working on my designs I love hair braiding, working out, and photography.
