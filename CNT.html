Digital Casting is the Future

In the digital age everyone now has the ability to have their own platform. This opens the door to endless opportunities to showcase talent. Acclaimed models, actors, directors, photographers, and stylists are showing off their skills to the world through their social media accounts. Social media traction turns into engagement which then becomes an opportunity for individuals to make a living from posting. However, when it comes to making a cross over from a social media career to a professional one a person will still have to go through the process of casting calls and callbacks. Just as a person can use social media for personal use, one can also use it to create fraudulent accounts for the purpose of extortion. Therefore social media is not a viable platform for verifying casting information posted digitally.

Digital casting refers to the process of individuals creating an online presence in order to secure talent opportunities. Talent profiles typically include photographs, a resume, and videos detailing a person's skill set. Unfortunately, individuals that are newer to the entertainment industry often get caught up in scams or fraudulent castings. To combat this, users can do their own research to verify information to protect their time and for their personal safety. However, the idea of a digital casting is still new to a lot of potential talent, which helped provide the basis for the problem statement:

How might we make a digital casting experience more convenient, safe, and accessible to anyone who could benefit from using it?

The solution is to design an app that enables its users access to verified casting information and opportunities to work within the entertainment industry. The resulting app, Cast Now: Talent is used to promote and share talent opportunities with industry professionals. The app is structured for both paid work and collaborations. It offers support for personal safety and encourages professionalism through ratings and review. Furthermore, users will be able to promote themselves with a digital portfolio and share their work and results to connect with industry professionals easily.

Outcomes and Lessons

 While I only focused on a few key features of this app, I would like to build out other key aspects. When it comes to talent, I would want to consider how to incorporate wellness tips to make sure that a user is at their best when it comes to a casting. I would also like to offer a premium version of the app where talent can request legal advice for contracts from entertainment lawyers who are paired with the platform. Finally I would like to look into unions for talent and see what type of benefits I can recreate digitally for the platform.

I would especially like to conduct research on the side of brands. Although marketing is overall more inclusive at this point in time, it is not a surprise that beauty standards are constantly changing. I would like to conduct research to see exactly how to build an intuitive design to help recruit and properly market opportunities.

Overall, the CastNow Talent app trained my eye to be more intentional with my design choices. Through the multiple iterations for the prototype, I had to step back and make sure each design decision I was making was appealing, functional and consistent with the app as a whole. This was also the first prototype I created in Figma which proved to be a great and versatile design tool.
